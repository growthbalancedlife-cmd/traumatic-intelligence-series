What Is Traumatic Intelligence?

Traumatic Intelligence is the ability to understand, integrate, and apply the lessons embedded in trauma — not as wounds to hide, but as wisdom to wield. This series bridges neuroscience, behavioral science, and lived experience into a structured learning path.

Whether you're beginning your own healing journey, supporting clients as a practitioner, or training others as a teacher — there is a tier designed for your level.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this series designed for?

This series is designed for three distinct audiences: individuals seeking personal healing and growth (Student), licensed or practicing wellness professionals (Practitioner), and educators or trainers who facilitate others (Teacher).

What format is the content delivered in?

All tiers are delivered digitally via Gumroad. You receive immediate access to your materials upon purchase — no waiting, no scheduled sessions required.

Is there a difference between the Practitioner and Teacher tiers beyond content?

Yes. The Teacher tier includes a licensing component that allows you to deliver the Traumatic Intelligence framework in group, workshop, or organizational settings. The Practitioner tier is for 1-on-1 client application only.

Can therapy practices purchase multiple Practitioner licenses?

Yes. B2B licensing for therapy practices is available. Contact us directly at the link below for group pricing.

Do I need a clinical background to benefit from this series?

No clinical background is required for the Student tier. The Practitioner tier assumes you are actively working with clients in a coaching or therapeutic capacity.
