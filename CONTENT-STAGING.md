## 📦 Tier Descriptions (FINALIZED)

### The Student — $57
**Who it's for:** The individual beginning their healing journey

**Full description:**
Trauma leaves marks — not just in memory, but in the body, in your patterns, in the way you move through the world. The Student tier is for the person who is ready to stop wondering *why* they react the way they do and start building a real relationship with their own intelligence.

This is your entry point into the Traumatic Intelligence framework. You'll move through the core concepts at your own pace, with guided reflection prompts, exercises, and a digital workbook designed to help you turn lived experience into self-understanding. No clinical background required. No performance. Just you, doing the honest work.

**What you get:**
- Core TI framework modules
- Self-paced digital access
- Reflection prompts & exercises
- Digital workbook
- Lifetime access

**Key transformation promise:** Stop reacting to your trauma. Start understanding it.

---

### The Practitioner — $127
**Who it's for:** Coaches, therapists, and healing professionals

**Full description:**
You already know how to hold space for others. The Practitioner tier gives you a structured framework for bringing Traumatic Intelligence into your client work — so the insights you've been offering intuitively now have language, architecture, and application behind them.

This tier is built for people who work 1-on-1 with clients in coaching, therapy, facilitation, or healing-centered practice. You'll receive everything in the Student tier plus client application frameworks, session integration guides, and a B2B practice toolkit designed for professional use. A certificate of completion is included.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Student tier
- Client application frameworks
- Session integration guides
- B2B practice toolkit
- Practitioner certificate of completion

**Key transformation promise:** Give your intuitive practice a structural foundation.

---

### The Teacher — $397
**Who it's for:** Educators, trainers, and organizational leaders

**Full description:**
Some people learn this work for themselves. Some apply it with clients. And some are called to carry it further — into rooms, organizations, training programs, and communities where it can change how entire groups of people understand themselves.

The Teacher tier is for facilitators, curriculum designers, organizational trainers, and leaders who want to deliver the Traumatic Intelligence framework in group settings. You receive the full curriculum, workshop slide decks, a facilitation guide, and a license for group delivery — plus direct support from Vision:MB as you build your program.

**What you get:**
- Everything in Practitioner tier
- Full curriculum & facilitation guide
- Workshop slide decks
- Group delivery license
- Direct Vision:MB support

**Key transformation promise:** Build a program that carries this work into the world.
